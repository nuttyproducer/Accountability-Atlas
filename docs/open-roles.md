# Open Roles

This project depends on small, focused volunteer contributions.

---

## Founding technical lead

### Responsibilities

- help choose MVP architecture;
- review technical PRs;
- prevent over-engineering;
- protect security and privacy principles;
- mentor contributors.

### Ideal background

- frontend/backend experience;
- open-source comfort;
- security-aware mindset;
- calm communication.

---

## Frontend contributor

### Responsibilities

- build static pages;
- implement accessible components;
- help with responsive design;
- create country page templates;
- implement source cards and action cards.

### Useful skills

- React, Vue, Svelte, Astro, or similar;
- HTML/CSS accessibility;
- Markdown/MDX;
- design-system thinking.

---

## Data/content model contributor

### Responsibilities

- define schemas for sources, countries, organizations, legal cases, actions;
- help structure content as JSON/YAML/Markdown;
- support future dossier generator.

### Useful skills

- JSON/YAML;
- data modeling;
- PostgreSQL later;
- content workflows.

---

## UX/UI designer

### Responsibilities

- calm visual direction;
- landing page wireframes;
- country page layout;
- mobile-first flows;
- accessibility review;
- social card templates.

---

## Research/source methodology contributor

### Responsibilities

- source hierarchy;
- claim categorization;
- evidence labels;
- country page research;
- organization directory research;
- correction process.

---

## Legal/human-rights reviewer

### Responsibilities

- review legal language;
- review genocide/war-crimes terminology;
- prevent overclaiming;
- advise on action templates;
- help separate allegations, findings, warrants, and judgments.

---

## Security/privacy reviewer

### Responsibilities

- review architecture;
- advise on analytics;
- advise on data minimization;
- flag unsafe features;
- help define future threat model.

---

## Communications contributor

### Responsibilities

- public copy;
- call for collaborators;
- newsletter drafts;
- social posts;
- press page;
- tone consistency.

---

## Translator

### Responsibilities

- translate approved public copy;
- preserve legal nuance;
- flag culturally unsafe wording;
- review machine translation outputs.

---

## Project coordinator

### Responsibilities

- issue organization;
- meeting notes;
- decision log;
- contributor onboarding;
- follow-up reminders;
- documentation hygiene.

---

## How to apply

Open an issue or contact the project coordinator with:

- role you are interested in;
- skills/background;
- availability;
- examples of work if available;
- whether you prefer public or private contribution.

Do not share sensitive personal information.
