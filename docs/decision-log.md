## 2026-07-09 — Working name selected

**Decision:**  
Use “Accountability Atlas” as the working project name.

**Context:**  
The platform needs a serious, institution-grade name that works for evidence, maps, legal tracking, country scorecards, and future expansion.

**Options considered:**

1. Accountability Atlas
2. Civic Witness
3. Evidence for Protection
4. Civilian Protection Index
5. Atrocity Accountability Hub

**Chosen option:**  
Accountability Atlas.

**Reason:**  
It is calm, global, credible, map-friendly, and flexible enough for multiple crisis modules.

**Risks:**  
It is broad and may need strong tagline support.

**Review date:**  
Before public domain purchase or public launch.

**Owner:**  
Founder/product steward.

## 2026-07-09 — MVP will not accept witness submissions

**Decision:**  
The first MVP will not accept sensitive witness submissions, raw evidence uploads, or private testimony.

**Context:**  
Witness workflows are high-risk and require expert review.

**Options considered:**

1. Build submissions immediately
2. Use a simple public form
3. Delay submissions until review

**Chosen option:**  
Delay submissions until security, privacy, legal, and human-rights review.

**Reason:**  
Avoid exposing vulnerable people and avoid unsafe data handling.

**Risks:**  
The platform may feel less powerful at launch, but it will be safer and more credible.

**Review date:**  
After static MVP and advisory review.

**Owner:**  
Core team.
